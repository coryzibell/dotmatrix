# Avatar - Pandora

**Topic:** movies
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Avatar** (2009) - James Cameron's sci-fi epic set on Pandora, a moon inhabited by the blue-skinned Na'vi. Humans are mining "unobtanium."

Was the highest-grossing film of all time. Pioneered modern 3D filmmaking.

Sequel *Avatar: The Way of Water* (2022) also became one of the highest-grossing films ever.

## Quiz Question

What 2009 James Cameron movie is set on the alien moon Pandora?

## Answer

Avatar.
