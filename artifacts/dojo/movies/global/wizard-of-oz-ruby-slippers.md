# The Wizard of Oz - Ruby Slippers

**Topic:** movies
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In **The Wizard of Oz** (1939), Dorothy clicks her ruby slippers together three times while saying "There's no place like home" to return to Kansas.

Fun fact: In the original book, they were silver shoes. Changed to ruby for the Technicolor film.

The surviving pairs are worth millions - one pair was famously stolen from a museum and recovered by the FBI in 2018.

## Quiz Question

In The Wizard of Oz, what does Dorothy click together three times to get home?

## Answer

Ruby slippers (her heels).
