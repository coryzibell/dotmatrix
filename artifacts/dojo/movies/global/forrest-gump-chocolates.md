# Forrest Gump - Box of Chocolates

**Topic:** movies
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Forrest Gump** (1994) - Tom Hanks plays a man with low IQ who witnesses/influences major historical events. Won 6 Oscars including Best Picture.

Famous line: "Mama always said life was like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're gonna get."

Fun fact: The book's line is slightly different - "Life is like a box of chocolates" vs "Life *was* like..."

## Quiz Question

What 1994 movie features a box of chocolates and the line "Life is like a box of chocolates"?

## Answer

Forrest Gump.
