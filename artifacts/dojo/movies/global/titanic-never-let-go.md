# Titanic - "I'll Never Let Go"

**Topic:** movies
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In **Titanic** (1997), Rose promises Jack "I'll never let go" as he freezes in the Atlantic. She then immediately lets go and he sinks.

The irony has been memed endlessly. Also spawned the "there was room on the door" debate (Mythbusters confirmed both could've fit, but buoyancy was the issue).

## Quiz Question

In the movie Titanic, what does Rose promise Jack she'll never do?

## Answer

Let go ("I'll never let go, Jack").
