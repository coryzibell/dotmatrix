# Samurai Jack - Aku

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Aku** is the shape-shifting Master of Darkness in *Samurai Jack* (Cartoon Network, 2001-2004, Adult Swim 2017).

Voiced by the legendary Mako (and later Greg Baldwin). Sends Jack to the future to prevent him from using his magic sword to defeat Aku.

Famous opening: "Long ago in a distant land, I, Aku, the shape-shifting Master of Darkness, unleashed an unspeakable evil!"

Created by Genndy Tartakovsky.

## Quiz Question

In Samurai Jack, what is the name of the shape-shifting demon who sends Jack to the future?

## Answer

Aku.
