# Codename: Kids Next Door - Numbuh 1

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Numbuh 1** (Nigel Uno) is the bald, sunglasses-wearing leader of Sector V in *Codename: Kids Next Door* (Cartoon Network, 2002-2008).

Sector V members:
- Numbuh 1 (Nigel) - leader
- Numbuh 2 (Hoagie) - pilot/inventor
- Numbuh 3 (Kuki) - medic, loves Rainbow Monkeys
- Numbuh 4 (Wally) - combat
- Numbuh 5 (Abby) - second-in-command

The show was about kids fighting adult tyranny with 2x4 technology.

## Quiz Question

In Codename: Kids Next Door, what is the numbuh of the leader of Sector V?

## Answer

Numbuh 1 (Nigel Uno).
