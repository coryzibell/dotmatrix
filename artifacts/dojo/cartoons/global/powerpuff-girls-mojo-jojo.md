# The Powerpuff Girls - Mojo Jojo

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Mojo Jojo** is the main villain in *The Powerpuff Girls* (Cartoon Network, 1998-2005). A green-skinned chimpanzee with an enlarged, exposed brain protected by a helmet.

Once Professor Utonium's lab assistant "Jojo," he was mutated by the same Chemical X accident that created the Powerpuff Girls.

Known for his redundant, repetitive way of speaking that says the same thing multiple times.

## Quiz Question

In The Powerpuff Girls, what is the name of the green villain who is a chimpanzee with an exposed brain?

## Answer

Mojo Jojo.
