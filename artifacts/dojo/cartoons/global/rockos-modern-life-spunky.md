# Rocko's Modern Life - Spunky

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Spunky** is Rocko's pet dog in *Rocko's Modern Life* (Nickelodeon, 1993-1996). A dim-witted but lovable bull terrier.

Spunky was known for eating garbage and having a romantic obsession with a mop.

The show was created by Joe Murray and was famous for sneaking adult humor past the censors.

## Quiz Question

In Rocko's Modern Life, what is the name of Rocko's pet dog?

## Answer

Spunky.
