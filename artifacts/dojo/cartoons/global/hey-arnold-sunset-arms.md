# Hey Arnold! - Sunset Arms

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

The **Sunset Arms** is the boarding house where Arnold lives with his grandparents Phil and Gertie in *Hey Arnold!* (Nickelodeon, 1996-2004).

Located in the fictional city of Hillwood. Full of quirky boarders like Mr. Hyunh, Ernie, and Oskar.

Arnold's room is in the attic with the iconic skylight.

## Quiz Question

In Hey Arnold!, what is the name of the boarding house where Arnold lives with his grandparents?

## Answer

The Sunset Arms.
