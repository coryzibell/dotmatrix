# Johnny Bravo

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Johnny Bravo** (Cartoon Network, 1997-2004) - muscular, dimwitted guy with Elvis-style blonde pompadour who constantly fails at picking up women.

Lives with his mom. Famous for his karate poses and catchphrases like "Do the monkey with me!" and "Man, I'm pretty."

Created by Van Partible. Part of Cartoon Network's original lineup alongside Dexter's Lab and Powerpuff Girls.

## Quiz Question

What Cartoon Network show features a boy named Johnny with a tall blonde flattop who constantly tries to impress women?

## Answer

Johnny Bravo.
