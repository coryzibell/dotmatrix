# Dexter's Laboratory - Dee Dee

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Dee Dee** is Dexter's tall, blonde, ballet-loving older sister in *Dexter's Laboratory* (Cartoon Network, 1996-2003).

Famous catchphrase: "Ooooh, what does THIS button do?" before inevitably destroying something in Dexter's secret lab.

Created by Genndy Tartakovsky (who also made Samurai Jack and Primal).

## Quiz Question

In Dexter's Laboratory, what is the name of Dexter's annoying sister who always breaks into his lab?

## Answer

Dee Dee.
