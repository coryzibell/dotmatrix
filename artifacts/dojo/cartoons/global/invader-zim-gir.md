# Invader Zim - GIR

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**GIR** is Zim's malfunctioning robot sidekick in *Invader Zim* (Nickelodeon, 2001-2002, revived 2019).

Disguises as a green dog. Obsessed with tacos, waffles, piggies, and the Scary Monkey Show. The "G" doesn't stand for anything.

Created by Jhonen Vasquez. The show was dark and weird for Nickelodeon, developed a cult following.

## Quiz Question

In Invader Zim, what is the name of Zim's malfunctioning robot sidekick?

## Answer

GIR.
