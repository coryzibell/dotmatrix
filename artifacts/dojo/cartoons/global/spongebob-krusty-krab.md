# SpongeBob - Krusty Krab

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

The **Krusty Krab** is the fast food restaurant in Bikini Bottom where SpongeBob works as a fry cook. Owned by Mr. Krabs.

Serves the secret-recipe Krabby Patty. Rival restaurant is the Chum Bucket, run by Plankton.

The building is shaped like a lobster trap.

## Quiz Question

In SpongeBob SquarePants, what is the name of the restaurant where SpongeBob works?

## Answer

The Krusty Krab.
