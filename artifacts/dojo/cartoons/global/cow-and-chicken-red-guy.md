# Cow and Chicken - The Red Guy

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**The Red Guy** is the main antagonist in *Cow and Chicken* (Cartoon Network, 1997-1999). A red devil with an exposed butt who appears in countless disguises.

His aliases include: The Devil, Officer Pantsoffski, Mrs. Barederriere, Lance Sackless, Baron Von Neinlederhosen, and many more - all referencing his lack of pants.

Also appeared in the spinoff *I Am Weasel*.

## Quiz Question

In Cow and Chicken, what is the name of the red devil character who torments the siblings?

## Answer

The Red Guy.
