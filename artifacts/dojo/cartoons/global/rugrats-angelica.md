# Rugrats - Angelica

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Angelica Pickles** is Tommy's spoiled 3-year-old cousin in *Rugrats* (Nickelodeon, 1991-2004).

Constantly bullies the babies, calls them "dumb babies," but also sometimes helps them. Has a doll named Cynthia.

One of the most iconic cartoon antagonists of the '90s.

## Quiz Question

In Rugrats, what is the name of Tommy Pickles' mean cousin who always bullies the babies?

## Answer

Angelica Pickles.
