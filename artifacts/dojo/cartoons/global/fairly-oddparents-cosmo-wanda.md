# The Fairly OddParents - Cosmo and Wanda

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Cosmo** (green, dim) and **Wanda** (pink, smart) are Timmy Turner's fairy godparents in *The Fairly OddParents* (Nickelodeon, 2001-2017).

They grant wishes to miserable kids. Later seasons added their baby Poof and dog Sparky.

Created by Butch Hartman, who also made Danny Phantom.

## Quiz Question

In The Fairly OddParents, what are the names of Timmy Turner's two fairy godparents?

## Answer

Cosmo and Wanda.
