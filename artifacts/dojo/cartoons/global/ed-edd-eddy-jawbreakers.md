# Ed, Edd n Eddy - Jawbreakers

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Jawbreakers** are the candy the three Eds constantly scheme to buy in *Ed, Edd n Eddy* (Cartoon Network, 1999-2009).

Every episode featured the Eds running scams on the neighborhood kids to get quarters for giant jawbreakers from the candy store. The schemes almost always backfired.

Longest-running original Cartoon Network series (10 years).

## Quiz Question

In Ed, Edd n Eddy, what candy do the Eds constantly scheme to buy?

## Answer

Jawbreakers.
