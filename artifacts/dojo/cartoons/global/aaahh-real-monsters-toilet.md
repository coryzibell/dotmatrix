# Aaahh!!! Real Monsters - Toilet Trivia

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Aaahh!!! Real Monsters* (Nickelodeon, 1994-1997):
- The monsters use a **toilet** as a portal to get to the human world to scare people
- Their actual toilet at Monster Academy (under the city dump) is a **washing machine**

The show followed Ickis, Oblina, and Krumm learning to be scary at the Gromble's monster school.

## Quiz Question

In Aaahh!!! Real Monsters, what do the monsters use as a toilet at their school under the dump?

## Answer

A washing machine (the toilet is their portal to the human world).
