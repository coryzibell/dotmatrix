# Courage the Cowardly Dog - Nowhere

**Topic:** cartoons
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Nowhere** is the town where Courage lives in *Courage the Cowardly Dog* (Cartoon Network, 1999-2002). It's in "the middle of Nowhere, Kansas."

Courage lives with elderly couple Muriel (kind) and Eustace Bagge (mean) on their isolated farmhouse. The show constantly plays on the "middle of nowhere" joke.

Known for being surprisingly dark and creepy for a kids' show.

## Quiz Question

In Courage the Cowardly Dog, what is the name of the town in the middle of Nowhere where Courage lives?

## Answer

Nowhere (the town is literally called Nowhere, Kansas).
