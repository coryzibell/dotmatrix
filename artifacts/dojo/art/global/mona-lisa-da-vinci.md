# Mona Lisa - Leonardo da Vinci

**Topic:** art
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Leonardo da Vinci** painted the Mona Lisa (1503-1519). It hangs in the Louvre in Paris.

Common mix-ups:
- Michelangelo: Sistine Chapel ceiling, statue of David
- Picasso: 20th century, Cubism, Guernica
- Raphael: School of Athens

All three (Leonardo, Michelangelo, Raphael) were Renaissance masters and also Ninja Turtles.

## Quiz Question

What artist painted the Mona Lisa?

## Answer

Leonardo da Vinci.
