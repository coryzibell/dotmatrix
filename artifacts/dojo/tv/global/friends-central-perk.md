# Friends - Central Perk

**Topic:** tv
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Central Perk** is the coffee shop in *Friends* (1994-2004) where the six main characters hang out. Features the iconic orange couch.

Gunther worked there and had an unrequited crush on Rachel. The name is a pun on Central Park.

## Quiz Question

In Friends, what is the name of the coffee shop where the gang hangs out?

## Answer

Central Perk.
