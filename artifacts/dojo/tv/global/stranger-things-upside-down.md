# Stranger Things - The Upside Down

**Topic:** tv
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Stranger Things** (Netflix, 2016-present) - set in Hawkins, Indiana in the 1980s. Kids discover a parallel dimension called the Upside Down filled with monsters.

Features Eleven (a girl with psychic powers), Demogorgons, and heavy '80s nostalgia (Dungeons & Dragons, synth soundtrack, Stephen King/Spielberg vibes).

Created by the Duffer Brothers.

## Quiz Question

What Netflix series follows a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana dealing with a parallel dimension called the Upside Down?

## Answer

Stranger Things.
