# Breaking Bad - Heisenberg

**Topic:** tv
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Breaking Bad** (2008-2013) follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with cancer who turns to cooking meth with former student Jesse Pinkman. His alias "Heisenberg" references the physicist Werner Heisenberg.

Created by Vince Gilligan. Widely considered one of the greatest TV dramas ever made.

## Quiz Question

What TV show features a chemistry teacher who becomes a drug manufacturer under the alias "Heisenberg"?

## Answer

Breaking Bad.
