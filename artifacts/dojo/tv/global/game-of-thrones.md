# Game of Thrones

**Topic:** tv
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Game of Thrones** (HBO, 2011-2019) - fantasy epic featuring dragons, White Walkers, and the Iron Throne. Based on George R.R. Martin's *A Song of Ice and Fire* books.

8 seasons. Famous for shocking deaths, political intrigue, and a controversial final season.

Prequel series *House of the Dragon* started in 2022.

## Quiz Question

What HBO series features dragons, White Walkers, and the Iron Throne?

## Answer

Game of Thrones.
