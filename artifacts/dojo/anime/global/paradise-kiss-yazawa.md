# Paradise Kiss - Yazawa Arts

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/josei)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Paradise Kiss* (2005), Yukari models for students at **Yazawa Arts** (Yazagaku), a vocational fashion design school.

**The setup:**
- "Paradise Kiss" is a fashion brand created by a group of Yazawa Arts students
- Led by the eccentric, talented George Koizumi
- Yukari (nicknamed "Caroline") is scouted off the street to model their designs
- They're preparing for the school's fashion show

**Characters:**
- George - designer, main love interest, glamorous and emotionally distant
- Miwako - cute pink-haired girl
- Arashi - punk rocker, Miwako's boyfriend
- Isabella - transgender woman, makes the patterns

Created by **Ai Yazawa** (also created *Nana*). The series explores fashion, ambition, first love, and growing up. More mature/josei tone than typical shoujo.

## Quiz Question

In Paradise Kiss, what fashion school does Yukari model for?

## Answer

Yazawa Arts (Yazagaku).
