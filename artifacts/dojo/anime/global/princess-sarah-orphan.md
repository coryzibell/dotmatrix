# Princess Sarah - Sara's Fall

**Topic:** anime (World Masterpiece Theater / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Princess Sarah** (A Little Princess Sara / Shoukoujo Sara) was a 1985 anime adaptation of Frances Hodgson Burnett's "A Little Princess" that became hugely popular in the Philippines.

**The story:**
- Sara Crewe is a wealthy girl attending Miss Minchin's boarding school in London
- Her father Captain Crewe dies, leaving her penniless
- Miss Minchin, the cruel headmistress, forces Sara to work as a servant at the school she once attended as a star pupil
- Sara endures hardship but maintains her kindness and dignity

**Philippine popularity:**
- One of the most beloved anime in the Philippines
- Part of the World Masterpiece Theater series
- The rags-to-riches (then riches-to-rags) story resonated deeply with Filipino audiences
- Often cited alongside Cedie as iconic tearjerker anime

## Quiz Question

In the anime "Princess Sarah" (A Little Princess Sara), which was hugely popular in the Philippines, what happens to Sara that causes her to go from wealthy student to servant at her own school?

## Answer

Her father dies, leaving her orphaned and penniless. The headmistress forces her to become a servant.
