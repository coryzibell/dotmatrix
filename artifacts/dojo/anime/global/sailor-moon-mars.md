# Sailor Moon - Sailor Mars

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Sailor Mars** (Rei Hino) is the Sailor Guardian associated with the planet Mars.

**Character details:**
- Shrine maiden at Hikawa Shrine
- Has psychic/spiritual abilities even without transforming
- Fire-based attacks ("Fire Soul," "Burning Mandala")
- Serious, hot-tempered personality
- Often clashes with Usagi (they bicker constantly but are close friends)

**In the dub:** Called Raye Hino, attacks renamed "Mars Fire Ignite"

**Powers tied to Mars:**
- Fire (Mars = Roman god of war, associated with fire/passion)
- Ofuda (paper talismans) for spiritual attacks
- Precognition through fire reading

She's often considered the most mature and serious of the inner scouts.

## Quiz Question

In Sailor Moon, which Sailor Guardian is associated with Mars?

## Answer

Sailor Mars (Rei Hino).
