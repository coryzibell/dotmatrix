# Crayon Shin-chan - Butt Dance

**Topic:** anime (comedy / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Shin-chan's Buri Buri Dance** is his signature move.

**The move:**
- Also called "Ass Dance" or "Butt Wiggle"
- Shin-chan drops his pants and wiggles his bare bottom
- Does it to shock, embarrass, or annoy adults
- Often accompanied by the "Buri Buri" chant
- One of the most iconic (and controversial) gags in the show

**The character:**
- Shinnosuke "Shin-chan" Nohara is a 5-year-old boy
- Known for crude humor and inappropriate behavior
- Constantly embarrasses his mother Misae
- Despite vulgarity, the show has heart and satire

**Philippine airing:**
- Popular but sometimes edited for Philippine TV due to crude content
- Kids loved the naughty humor
