# Doraemon - 4D Pocket

**Topic:** anime (children's / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Doraemon's 4-Dimensional Pocket** is his signature feature.

**The pocket:**
- Circular/round shape on his belly
- White in color against his blue body
- Contains a 4-dimensional space inside
- Can hold infinite items from the future
- Where he pulls out all his gadgets

**Famous gadgets from the pocket:**
- Anywhere Door (Dokodemo Door)
- Take-copter (Bamboo-copter)
- Time Machine
- Small Light (shrinking flashlight)
- Translation Konjac

**The show:**
- Doraemon is a robot cat from the future
- Sent back to help Nobita (a lazy schoolboy)
- Massively popular across Asia including Philippines
- Created by Fujiko F. Fujio

## Quiz Question

In Doraemon, what shape is the pocket on Doraemon's belly where he pulls out gadgets from the future?

## Answer

Circle/round (the 4-dimensional pocket).
