# Inuyasha - Tessaiga

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/action)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Tessaiga** (also romanized as Tetsusaiga) is Inuyasha's sword - the "Iron-Crushing Fang."

**Details:**
- Forged from a fang of Inuyasha's father, the great dog demon Inu no Taisho
- Looks like a rusty katana in dormant form
- Transforms into a massive fang-blade when wielded by Inuyasha
- Can only be used by someone who wants to protect humans (why Sesshomaru can't wield it)

**Signature attacks:**
- Wind Scar (Kaze no Kizu) - main attack
- Backlash Wave (Bakuryuuha) - reflects enemy attacks
- Adamant Barrage - later upgrade

His brother Sesshomaru inherited Tenseiga (healing sword) and was bitter about not getting Tessaiga.

## Quiz Question

In Inuyasha, what is the name of Inuyasha's sword?

## Answer

Tessaiga (Tetsusaiga) - the Iron-Crushing Fang.
