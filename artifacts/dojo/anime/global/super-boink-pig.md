# Super Boink - Pig Transformation

**Topic:** anime (magical girl / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Super Boink** was the Filipino title for *Tonde Buurin* (Flying Pig), a comedic magical girl anime that aired in the Philippines.

**The premise:**
- Karin Kokubu is a regular girl who wants to become a beautiful magical girl
- Instead, she's given powers that transform her into a pig
- She fights evil as a super-powered pig, much to her embarrassment
- The comedy comes from her wanting glamour but getting... pork

**Why it worked:**
- Parody of the magical girl genre
- The contrast between expectations and reality was hilarious
- Popular in the Philippines for its humor

The title "Super Boink" was a creative Filipino localization.

## Quiz Question

In the magical girl anime "Super Boink" (the Filipino title for Tonde Buurin), the main character transforms into what animal to fight evil?

## Answer

A pig.
