# Cardcaptor Sakura - Sealing Wand

**Topic:** anime (magical girl / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**The Sealing Wand** is Sakura's primary magical tool in Cardcaptor Sakura.

**Details:**
- Used to capture and seal Clow Cards
- Transforms from a small key when Sakura recites her incantation
- Originally pink with a bird-like head design
- Later transforms into the Star Wand when Sakura converts the Clow Cards to Sakura Cards

**The incantation (English dub):**
"Key of Clow, power of magic, power of light, surrender the wand, the force ignite! Release!"

**Japanese version:**
"Sealed key... Release!"

The wand is essential - without it, Sakura cannot capture or use the cards.

## Quiz Question

In Cardcaptor Sakura, what magical item does Sakura use to capture the Clow Cards?

## Answer

The Sealing Wand (also called the Star Wand / her magical staff/rod/baton).
