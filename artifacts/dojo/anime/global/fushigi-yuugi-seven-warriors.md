# Fushigi Yuugi - Seven Celestial Warriors

**Topic:** anime (shoujo / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Fushigi Yuugi** (Mysterious Play) featured the quest to gather celestial warriors.

**The setup:**
- Miaka is the Priestess of Suzaku
- She must gather 7 celestial warriors to summon the beast god Suzaku
- Each warrior has a character mark on their body that glows

**The Seven Suzaku Warriors:**
1. Tamahome - forehead mark, martial artist, love interest
2. Hotohori - neck mark, the Emperor, swordsman
3. Nuriko - chest mark, super strength, crossdresser
4. Chichiri - knee mark, monk, teleportation
5. Tasuki - forearm mark, fire powers, bandit
6. Mitsukake - palm mark, healer
7. Chiriko - foot mark, genius child

The rival Priestess of Seiryuu (Yui) also had to gather 7 warriors of her own.

## Quiz Question

In Fushigi Yuugi, which aired in the Philippines, how many celestial warriors does Miaka need to gather to summon the beast god Suzaku?

## Answer

Seven (7) celestial warriors.
