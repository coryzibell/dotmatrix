# Kodocha - Sana's Job

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Kodocha* (Kodomo no Omocha / Child's Toy, 1996-1998), Sana Kurata is a **child actress and TV star** who hosts a variety show called "Child's Toy."

Despite being in elementary school, she's already:
- Famous across Japan
- Has her own manager (Rei Sagami)
- Lives with her eccentric novelist mother
- Deals with paparazzi and fame

**The premise:** Sana tries to reform Akito Hayama, the classroom delinquent who leads the boys in terrorizing their teacher. Their antagonistic relationship evolves into something deeper.

**Tone:** The show is famous for its manic, hyperactive comedy (Sana talks a mile a minute) but also tackles surprisingly heavy themes:
- Child abuse
- Abandonment
- Divorce
- Depression

Created by Miho Obana. Known for its rapid-fire humor and emotional whiplash.

## Quiz Question

In Kodocha, what job makes Sana unusual for an elementary school student?

## Answer

She's a child actress/TV star who hosts a variety show.
