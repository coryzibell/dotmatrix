# Cardcaptor Sakura - Touya

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Touya Kinomoto** (Tori in English dub) is Sakura's older brother in *Cardcaptor Sakura*.

**Character traits:**
- Protective of Sakura despite teasing her constantly
- Calls her "kaijuu" (monster)
- Can see spirits/ghosts (inherited ability)
- Knows about Sakura's magical adventures but pretends not to
- Best friends with Yukito (Yue's borrowed form)

**Major plot point:** Touya eventually sacrifices his spiritual powers to save Yukito/Yue, who was fading away due to lack of magical energy. This shows his deep love for both his sister and Yukito.

**Relationship with Yukito:** The series implies (and the manga confirms) a romantic relationship between Touya and Yukito, though this was downplayed in some dubs.

## Quiz Question

In Cardcaptor Sakura, what is the name of Sakura's older brother?

## Answer

Touya Kinomoto.
