# Glass Mask - The Crimson Goddess

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Glass Mask* (Garasu no Kamen, 1984/2005), Maya Kitajima spends the series competing to play **The Crimson Goddess** (Kurenai Tennyo).

**The premise:**
- The Crimson Goddess is a legendary play written by the aging actress Chigusa Tsukikage
- The role is so demanding only one actress per generation can perform it
- Maya (poor but naturally gifted) and Ayumi Himekawa (wealthy, trained, beautiful) compete throughout the series to prove their worth

**Legacy:**
- One of the longest-running shoujo manga (started 1976, still technically ongoing)
- Considered THE definitive "acting/theater" shoujo
- Hugely influential on the genre
- Multiple anime adaptations (1984, 2005)

Created by Suzue Miuchi. The manga has been on hiatus multiple times, and fans have waited decades for the ending.

## Quiz Question

In Glass Mask, what role does Maya spend the series competing to play?

## Answer

The Crimson Goddess (Kurenai Tennyo).
