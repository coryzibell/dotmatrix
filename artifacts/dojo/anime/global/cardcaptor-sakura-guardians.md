# Cardcaptor Sakura - The Guardians

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Cardcaptor Sakura* (1998-2000), the two guardians of the Clow Cards are:

**Keroberos (Kero)** - Guardian of the Sun
- Borrowed form: Small yellow stuffed animal-like creature with wings
- True form: Majestic winged lion
- Personality: Loves video games and sweets, lazy but loyal

**Yue** - Guardian of the Moon
- Borrowed form: Yukito Tsukishiro (human, Touya's best friend, Sakura's crush)
- True form: Silver-haired angel with white wings
- Personality: Cold, serious, initially skeptical of Sakura

**Clow Reed** was the powerful sorcerer who *created* the cards and guardians, not a guardian himself. He's long dead by the time of the story but appears in flashbacks.

The guardians test Sakura to determine if she's worthy of becoming the cards' new master.

## Quiz Question

In Cardcaptor Sakura, what are the two guardians of the Clow Cards and what forms do they take?

## Answer

Keroberos (Kero) - stuffed animal / winged lion. Yue - Yukito (human) / silver-haired angel.
