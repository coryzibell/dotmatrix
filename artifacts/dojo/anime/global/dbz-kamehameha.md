# Dragon Ball Z - Kamehameha

**Topic:** anime (shounen / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Kamehameha** is Goku's signature energy attack in Dragon Ball/Dragon Ball Z.

**The technique:**
- Charged with cupped hands pulled back to the side
- User chants "Ka-me-ha-me-HA!" while charging
- Releases a powerful blue energy beam
- Also called the "Turtle Destruction Wave"

**Origin:**
- Invented by Master Roshi (the Turtle Hermit)
- Took Roshi 50 years to develop
- Young Goku copied it after seeing it once (showing his prodigy talent)
- Became his go-to finishing move throughout the series

**Philippine popularity:**
- Dragon Ball Z was massively popular in the Philippines
- Kids everywhere did the Kamehameha pose
- Aired on multiple Philippine networks

## Quiz Question

In Dragon Ball Z, what is the name of Goku's signature energy attack - the one he charges with cupped hands?

## Answer

Kamehameha (Kamehame Wave / Turtle Destruction Wave).
