# Slam Dunk - Sakuragi's Basketball

**Topic:** anime (sports / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Slam Dunk** was a massively popular basketball anime in the Philippines during the 90s.

**The premise:**
- Hanamichi Sakuragi is a hot-headed delinquent who's been rejected by 50 girls
- Haruko Akagi shows interest in him and asks if he likes basketball
- He joins the Shohoku basketball team to impress her
- Despite being a complete beginner, his athleticism makes him a natural
- He becomes a legitimate player and falls in love with the sport itself

**Philippine popularity:**
- Basketball is the #1 sport in the Philippines
- Slam Dunk was incredibly influential on Filipino basketball culture
- Many Filipino players cite it as inspiration
- The show's mix of comedy, drama, and sports action was perfect for Filipino audiences

## Quiz Question

In Slam Dunk, which was hugely popular in the Philippines, what sport does Hanamichi Sakuragi take up to impress a girl?

## Answer

Basketball.
