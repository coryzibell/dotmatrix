# Fushigi Yuugi - The Priestess of Suzaku

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Fushigi Yuugi* (1995), Miaka Yuki is transported into **The Universe of the Four Gods** (Shi Jin Ten Chi Sho), an ancient Chinese book.

Inside, she must become the **Priestess of Suzaku**:
- Gather seven celestial warriors (each marked with a character on their body)
- Summon the god Suzaku
- Receive three wishes

**The four gods/countries:**
- Suzaku (south, phoenix) - Miaka's side
- Seiryuu (east, dragon) - Yui's side
- Byakko (west, tiger)
- Genbu (north, turtle)

**Conflict:** Miaka's best friend Yui also gets pulled into the book and becomes the rival **Priestess of Seiryuu** after a tragic misunderstanding, putting them at war.

The series (by Yuu Watase) features a reverse harem of warriors protecting Miaka, with Tamahome as the main love interest.

## Quiz Question

In Fushigi Yuugi, what book does Miaka enter and what role must she fulfill?

## Answer

The Universe of the Four Gods. She must become the Priestess of Suzaku, gather seven warriors, and summon the god.
