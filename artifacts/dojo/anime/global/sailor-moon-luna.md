# Sailor Moon - Luna

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Sailor Moon*, **Luna** is the black cat who gives Usagi her transformation brooch and awakens her as Sailor Moon.

**Luna's details:**
- Black cat with a crescent moon mark on her forehead
- Guardian cat from the Moon Kingdom
- Can talk
- Serves as Usagi's advisor/mentor
- Often exasperated by Usagi's laziness and crying

**Other guardian cats:**
- **Artemis** - white male cat, Sailor Venus's guardian
- **Diana** - Luna and Artemis's daughter from the future (appears later)

Luna was in suspended animation on Earth for centuries, waiting to awaken the Sailor Guardians when the Dark Kingdom threatened. She found Usagi by accident when Usagi removed a bandage that was covering her moon mark.

## Quiz Question

In Sailor Moon, what is the name of the cat who gave Usagi her transformation brooch?

## Answer

Luna.
