# Magic Knight Rayearth - Three Knights

**Topic:** anime (magical girl / mecha / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Magic Knight Rayearth** features three girls transported to another world.

**The three Magic Knights:**
1. Hikaru Shidou - Fire knight, red armor, pilots Rayearth
2. Umi Ryuuzaki - Water knight, blue armor, pilots Celes
3. Fuu Hououji - Wind knight, green armor, pilots Windam

**The story:**
- Three girls from different schools meet on a field trip to Tokyo Tower
- A bright light transports them to the magical world of Cephiro
- They must become Magic Knights to save Princess Emeraude
- Each gains magic powers and eventually a Mashin (giant robot)

**CLAMP creation:**
- Made by the all-female manga group CLAMP
- Mixed magical girl and mecha genres
- Popular in the Philippines during the 90s

## Quiz Question

In Magic Knight Rayearth, how many girls are transported from Tokyo Tower to the magical world of Cephiro?

## Answer

Three (Hikaru, Umi, and Fuu).
