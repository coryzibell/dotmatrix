# Mojacko - Alien Creature

**Topic:** anime (children's / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Mojacko** is a children's anime featuring a cute alien creature.

**The character:**
- Mojacko is an orange alien/space creature
- From planet Moja (a "Moja" creature)
- Befriends a young boy named Sorao
- Has a round, cute appearance
- Can do various magical/alien things

**The show:**
- Aired in the Philippines during the 90s/2000s
- Light-hearted children's comedy
- Similar appeal to Doraemon (alien/robot friend helps kid)
- Popular with younger Filipino audiences

The simple, heartwarming premise made it a favorite for Filipino kids.

## Quiz Question

In Mojacko, what type of creature is the title character that befriends a young boy?

## Answer

An alien (space creature / Moja from planet Moja).
