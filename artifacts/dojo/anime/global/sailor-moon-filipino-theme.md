# Sailor Moon - Filipino Theme Song

**Topic:** anime (Filipino dub)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

The Filipino dub of Sailor Moon had its own original theme song, not a translation of "Moonlight Densetsu."

**Filipino opening:**
- Title: "Sana ay di na magwakas" (roughly "I hope it never ends")
- Performed by: Angelika Dela Cruz
- An original Filipino-language song created specifically for the dub
- Became iconic among Filipino Sailor Moon fans

This was common practice for Philippine anime dubs - creating original Filipino songs rather than translating Japanese themes.

## Quiz Question

In the Filipino Sailor Moon dub, what was the title of the iconic opening theme song performed by Angelika Dela Cruz?

## Answer

"Sana ay di na magwakas" (I hope it never ends).
