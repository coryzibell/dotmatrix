# Ghost Fighter - Spirit Gun

**Topic:** anime (shounen / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Spirit Gun** (Rei Gun) is Yusuke Urameshi's signature attack in Yu Yu Hakusho / Ghost Fighter.

**The technique:**
- Yusuke concentrates his spirit energy into his fingertip
- Points his finger like a gun
- Fires a blast of concentrated spirit energy
- Initially could only use it once per day due to limited spirit energy
- Gets stronger as the series progresses

**Variations:**
- Spirit Gun (basic)
- Spirit Shotgun (multiple blasts)
- Spirit Gun Mega (powered up version)

In the Filipino dub, Eugene (Yusuke) used this as his main attack throughout the series.

## Quiz Question

In Yuyu Hakusho (Ghost Fighter), what technique does Yusuke charge in his finger before firing?

Spirit Gun (Rei Gun / Ray Gun).
