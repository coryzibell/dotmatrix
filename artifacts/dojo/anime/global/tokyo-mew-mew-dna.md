# Tokyo Mew Mew - Endangered Animal DNA

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Tokyo Mew Mew* (2002), the main characters are merged with **endangered animal DNA** to gain their powers.

**The Mew Mews and their animals:**
- **Ichigo** (Pink) - Iriomote wildcat (critically endangered Japanese cat)
- **Mint** (Blue) - Blue lorikeet
- **Lettuce** (Green) - Finless porpoise
- **Pudding** (Yellow) - Golden lion tamarin
- **Zakuro** (Purple) - Grey wolf

The endangered species theme ties into the show's environmental message - they fight aliens who want to reclaim Earth because humans have damaged the environment.

Ichigo gets cat ears and a tail when transformed (or when flustered/embarrassed), reflecting her Iriomote wildcat DNA. Her weapon is the Strawberry Bell.

Known as "Mew Mew Power" in the heavily edited English dub.

## Quiz Question

In Tokyo Mew Mew, what type of DNA gives the girls powers, and what species is Ichigo merged with?

## Answer

Endangered animal DNA. Ichigo is merged with the Iriomote wildcat.
