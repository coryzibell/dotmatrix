# Hunter x Hunter - Nen

**Topic:** anime (shounen)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Nen** is the power system in Hunter x Hunter.

**What it is:**
- The ability to manipulate life energy (aura)
- All living beings have aura, but most can't control it
- Hunters learn to control their Nen through training

**Basic techniques:**
- Ten - shroud yourself in aura
- Zetsu - hide your aura completely
- Ren - project your aura outward
- Hatsu - your personal Nen ability

**Nen types (categories):**
- Enhancer - strengthen body/objects
- Transmuter - change aura properties
- Conjurer - create objects from aura
- Emitter - project aura away from body
- Manipulator - control objects/people
- Specialist - unique abilities

One of the most complex and well-thought-out power systems in anime.

## Quiz Question

In Hunter x Hunter, what is the name of the power system that allows users to manipulate their life energy?

## Answer

Nen.
