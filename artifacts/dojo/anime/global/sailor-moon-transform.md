# Sailor Moon - Transformation Phrase

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Sailor Moon* (1992-1997), Usagi's transformation phrase is **"Moon Prism Power, Make Up!"**

She holds up her transformation brooch and shouts the phrase to trigger her magical girl transformation sequence.

**Transformation phrases evolved:**
- Season 1: "Moon Prism Power, Make Up!"
- Season 2 (R): "Moon Crystal Power, Make Up!"
- Season 3 (S): "Moon Cosmic Power, Make Up!"
- Season 4 (SuperS): "Moon Crisis, Make Up!"
- Season 5 (Stars): "Moon Eternal, Make Up!"

**Not to be confused with her catchphrase** (said AFTER transforming, before fighting):
"In the name of the moon, I'll punish you!" (Tsuki ni kawatte, oshioki yo!)

## Quiz Question

In Sailor Moon, what phrase does Usagi say to transform into Sailor Moon?

## Answer

"Moon Prism Power, Make Up!" (original series).
