# Voltes V - Marcos Ban

**Topic:** anime (super robot / Filipino history)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Voltes V** was a Japanese super robot anime that became enormously popular in the Philippines - until it was banned.

**The ban:**
- Ferdinand Marcos banned the show in 1979
- Only 5 episodes remained unaired
- Official reason: violence inappropriate for children
- Real reason (widely believed): The show's themes of revolution against a tyrannical empire were inspiring anti-Marcos sentiment among Filipino youth

**The story's parallels:**
- Voltes V features rebels fighting against the Boazanian Empire
- The empire is ruled by a tyrant
- The heroes unite to overthrow oppression
- These themes resonated strongly during Martial Law

After Marcos was ousted in 1986, the remaining episodes finally aired. Voltes V remains a cultural touchstone in the Philippines.

## Quiz Question

Which super robot anime was banned by Ferdinand Marcos in the Philippines with only 5 episodes remaining, allegedly because it was inspiring revolutionary sentiments among Filipino youth?

## Answer

Voltes V.
