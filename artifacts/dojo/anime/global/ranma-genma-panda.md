# Ranma ½ - Genma the Panda

**Topic:** anime (comedy / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Genma Saotome** is Ranma's father in Ranma ½.

**His curse:**
- Fell into the "Spring of Drowned Panda" at Jusenkyo
- Transforms into a giant panda when splashed with cold water
- Returns to human form with hot water
- Communicates via wooden signs he holds up while in panda form

**Character traits:**
- Greedy and cowardly
- Made many bad decisions that affect Ranma (like the multiple engagements)
- Often gets into fights with Ranma
- Lazy but was once a formidable martial artist
- Engaged Ranma to Akane to unite their martial arts schools

The panda gag was one of the show's most iconic running jokes.

## Quiz Question

In Ranma ½, what is the name of Ranma's father, who transforms into a panda when splashed with cold water?

## Answer

Genma Saotome.
