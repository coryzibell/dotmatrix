# Mermaid Melody Pichi Pichi Pitch - Singing Attacks

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Mermaid Melody Pichi Pichi Pitch* (2003), the mermaid princesses fight enemies by **singing** - they transform into idol singers and defeat villains with their songs.

**How it works:**
- Each mermaid princess has a **pearl** that allows transformation
- They use **microphones** powered by their pearls
- They perform pop songs that literally defeat/purify enemies
- Villains can't withstand the power of their music

The show is a magical girl + idol singer hybrid. The protagonist Lucia is the Pink Pearl Mermaid Princess who must gather the other six mermaid princesses (each representing a different ocean and pearl color).

**Notable aspect:** The anime features many original songs performed during battle sequences, making the soundtrack a significant part of the show's appeal.

Based on the manga by Pink Hanamori.
