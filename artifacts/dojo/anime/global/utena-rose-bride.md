# Revolutionary Girl Utena - The Rose Bride

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Revolutionary Girl Utena* (1997), Utena Tenjou fights duels to protect **Anthy Himemiya**, the **Rose Bride**.

**The dueling system:**
- The winner of each duel "possesses" the Rose Bride - Anthy is engaged to whoever wins
- Anthy is passed between duelists like a prize/object
- The ultimate goal is to gain "the power to revolutionize the world"
- Duels are fought in a surreal floating arena with an upside-down castle overhead

**Themes:**
- Deconstruction of fairy tales and prince/princess dynamics
- Utena wants to be a prince, not a princess (wears a boy's uniform)
- The Rose Bride system is revealed as deeply exploitative and sinister
- Heavy symbolism, surrealism, and social commentary throughout

Directed by Kunihiko Ikuhara (who also did Sailor Moon), known for dense symbolism and subversive storytelling.

## Quiz Question

In Revolutionary Girl Utena, what does Utena fight duels to protect, and what does the winner possess?

## Answer

She protects Anthy Himemiya, the Rose Bride. The winner possesses/is engaged to the Rose Bride.
