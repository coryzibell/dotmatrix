# Flame of Recca - Hokage Clan

**Topic:** anime (shounen / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Flame of Recca** features the Hokage ninja clan.

**The Hokage clan:**
- Ancient ninja clan that mastered flame techniques
- Recca Hanabishi is the son of the clan's leader
- The clan was destroyed 400 years ago
- Recca was sent through time as a baby to the present day
- "Hokage" means "fire shadow"

**Recca's powers:**
- Controls fire
- Can summon flame dragons (8 different dragons with unique abilities)
- Each dragon represents a former Hokage leader's spirit

The series was popular in the Philippines, featuring tournament-style battles and ninja action.

## Quiz Question

In Flame of Recca, what is the name of the ninja clan that Recca belongs to?

## Answer

The Hokage clan.
