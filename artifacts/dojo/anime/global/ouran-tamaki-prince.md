# Ouran High School Host Club - Tamaki

**Topic:** anime (shoujo)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Ouran High School Host Club*, Tamaki Suoh is the **Prince type** (or King type).

**His role:**
- Founder and president of the Host Club
- The romantic, charming gentleman who sweeps girls off their feet
- Uses roses, grand gestures, and flowery compliments
- Calls himself "King" of the club

**His character:**
- Half-French, half-Japanese
- Extremely dramatic and emotional
- Genuinely kind but completely oblivious
- Doesn't realize he's in love with Haruhi for most of the series
- Treats the club members as his "family"

He created the Host Club based on his belief that making girls happy is a noble pursuit - though his methods are over-the-top theatrical.

## Quiz Question

In Ouran High School Host Club, what is Tamaki's host type?

## Answer

Prince type (or King type) - the charming romantic gentleman.
