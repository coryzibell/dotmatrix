# Cedie - Little Lord Fauntleroy

**Topic:** anime (World Masterpiece Theater / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Cedie** (Shoukoujo Cedie) was a 1988 anime adaptation of Frances Hodgson Burnett's "Little Lord Fauntleroy" that became a beloved classic in the Philippines.

**The story:**
- Cedric "Cedie" Errol is a kind American boy living in New York with his mother
- He discovers he's the heir to his grandfather, the Earl of Dorincourt in England
- His cold, bitter grandfather eventually warms to Cedie's pure heart
- Classic rags-to-riches story with themes of family and kindness

**Philippine popularity:**
- One of the most iconic anime in the Philippines alongside Princess Sarah
- Both are Frances Hodgson Burnett adaptations
- Both are World Masterpiece Theater productions
- Known for making Filipino audiences cry

## Quiz Question

Little Lord Fauntleroy (by Frances Hodgson Burnett).
