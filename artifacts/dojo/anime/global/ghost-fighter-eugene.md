# Ghost Fighter - Eugene (Yusuke)

**Topic:** anime (Filipino dub)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Ghost Fighter** was the Filipino dub of *Yu Yu Hakusho* that aired in the Philippines during the 90s/2000s.

**Filipino character names:**
- Yusuke Urameshi → **Eugene**
- Kazuma Kuwabara → **Alfred**
- Kurama → **Dennis** (originally "Denise" - dubbers thought Kurama was female due to his appearance, changed mid-series when they realized he was male)
- Hiei → **Vincent**
- Botan → **Charlene**
- Koenma → **Jericho**
- Genkai → **Jeremiah**

The show was massively popular in the Philippines, airing on GMA Network.

## Quiz Question

In the Filipino dub of Yu Yu Hakusho, known as "Ghost Fighter" in the Philippines, what was Yusuke Urameshi's Filipino name?

## Answer

Eugene.
