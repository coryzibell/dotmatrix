# Sailor Moon - Tuxedo Mask

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/magical girl)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Sailor Moon*, Tuxedo Mask's civilian name is **Mamoru Chiba** (Darien Shields in the English dub).

**His role:**
- Usagi's destined love interest
- Throws roses and gives encouraging speeches during battles
- Past life: Prince Endymion of Earth, lover of Princess Serenity (Usagi)
- Future: King Endymion of Crystal Tokyo, father of Chibiusa

**His powers:**
- Rose throwing (distracts enemies)
- Psychic connection to Usagi
- Healing abilities (limited)
- Generally serves as support rather than main fighter

The name "Mamoru" means "to protect" in Japanese. He's often criticized by fans for being relatively useless in battles compared to the Sailor Guardians.
