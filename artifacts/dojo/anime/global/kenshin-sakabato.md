# Rurouni Kenshin - Sakabato

**Topic:** anime (shounen / Filipino era)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Sakabato** (reverse-blade sword) is Kenshin's signature weapon in Rurouni Kenshin.

**What makes it unique:**
- The sharp edge is on the inside (back) of the curve instead of the outside
- This makes it non-lethal - the blunt edge strikes opponents
- Symbolizes Kenshin's vow to never kill again
- He can still use his deadly Hiten Mitsurugi-ryÅ« techniques without killing

**Kenshin's past:**
- Was the legendary assassin "Hitokiri Battousai" during the Bakumatsu
- Killed many people during the revolution
- After the war, he vowed never to kill again
- Wanders as a rurouni (wanderer) seeking atonement

The sakabato represents his redemption and commitment to protecting life rather than taking it.

## Quiz Question

In Rurouni Kenshin, what is unique about Kenshin's reverse-blade sword (sakabato)?

The sharp edge is on the opposite side (inside curve), making it non-lethal. This reflects his vow to never kill again.
