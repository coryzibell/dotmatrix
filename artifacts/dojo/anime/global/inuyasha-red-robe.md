# Inuyasha - Red Robe

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/action)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

Inuyasha's iconic robe is **red** - specifically the **Robe of the Fire-Rat** (Hi-Nezumi no Koromo).

**Properties:**
- Made from the fur of a fire-rat
- Fire-resistant and provides protection
- Given to him by his human mother, Izayoi
- One of his few possessions from her

**His signature look:**
- Red haori (jacket) and hakama (pants)
- Long white/silver hair
- Dog ears (from his half-demon heritage)
- Barefoot
- Tessaiga sword at his hip

The red robe is so iconic that "red-clad" is often used to describe him in the series.

## Quiz Question

In Inuyasha, what color is Inuyasha's robe?

## Answer

Red (the Robe of the Fire-Rat).
