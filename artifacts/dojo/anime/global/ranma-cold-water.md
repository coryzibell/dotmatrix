# Ranma ½ - Cold Water Curse

**Topic:** anime (shoujo/comedy)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In *Ranma ½* (1989-1992), **cold water** triggers Ranma Saotome's transformation into a girl. **Hot water** changes him back to a boy.

**The curse origin:**
- Ranma fell into the "Spring of Drowned Girl" (Nyannīchuan) at Jusenkyo in China
- Jusenkyo is a cursed training ground with hundreds of springs
- Each spring drowns whoever falls in as whatever drowned there before

**Other cursed characters:**
- Genma (father) - Spring of Drowned Panda → turns into a panda
- Ryoga - Spring of Drowned Pig → turns into a small black pig
- Shampoo - Spring of Drowned Cat → turns into a cat
- Mousse - Spring of Drowned Duck → turns into a duck

The comedy comes from inconvenient splashes at the worst moments.

Created by Rumiko Takahashi.

## Quiz Question

In Ranma ½, what triggers Ranma's transformation into a girl?

## Answer

Cold water (hot water changes him back).
