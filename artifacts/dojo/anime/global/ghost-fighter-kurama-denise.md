# Ghost Fighter - Kurama's Name Change

**Topic:** anime (Filipino dub)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

In the Filipino dub of Yu Yu Hakusho (Ghost Fighter), Kurama's name famously changed mid-series.

**The story:**
- Filipino dubbers initially named Kurama "Denise"
- Kurama has long red hair and delicate, feminine features
- The dubbers assumed Kurama was female based on appearance
- When they realized Kurama was actually male, they quietly changed the name to "Dennis"
- This became a running joke among Filipino anime fans

This is one of the most famous dubbing errors in Philippine anime history.

## Quiz Question

In the Filipino dub of Ghost Fighter, why did the character Kurama's name change from "Denise" to "Dennis" partway through the series?

The dubbers initially thought Kurama was female due to his feminine appearance/long hair. When they realized he was male, they changed the name to Dennis.
