# Breaking Bad - Casa Tranquila

**Topic:** breaking-bad
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Casa Tranquila** is the nursing home where Hector "Tio" Salamanca lives after his stroke leaves him unable to speak or move, communicating only by ringing a bell.

It's where Hector ultimately kills Gustavo Fring by detonating a pipe bomb attached to his wheelchair. Gus walks out of the room with half his face blown off, straightens his tie, then collapses dead.

This happens in the season 4 finale "Face Off."

## Quiz Question

What is the name of the nursing home where Hector Salamanca killed Gus Fring with a wheelchair bomb?

## Answer

Casa Tranquila.
