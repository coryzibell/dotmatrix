# Breaking Bad - Baby Blue Finale

**Topic:** breaking-bad
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**"Baby Blue"** by Badfinger plays during the final scene of Breaking Bad's series finale "Felina."

The song plays as Walter White dies in the meth lab after killing the neo-Nazis with a remote-activated M60 machine gun hidden in his car trunk. The lyrics "Guess I got what I deserved" perfectly encapsulate Walt's arc.

He dies touching the lab equipment - the thing he truly loved - as police close in.

## Quiz Question

What song plays during Walter White's death in the Breaking Bad finale?

## Answer

"Baby Blue" by Badfinger.
