# Breaking Bad - Mr. Lambert

**Topic:** breaking-bad
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Mr. Lambert** is the fake identity Walter White assumes when he goes into hiding in New Hampshire with the help of Ed Galbraith (the "vacuum cleaner repair" disappearer).

The name "Lambert" is Skyler's maiden name - a subtle detail showing Walt can't fully let go of his family even while on the run.

Walt lives in an isolated cabin, growing a beard, with Ed as his only contact with the outside world.

## Quiz Question

What fake name does Walter White use when he goes into hiding in New Hampshire?

## Answer

Mr. Lambert (Skyler's maiden name).
