# Missing Letter in U.S. State Names

**Topic:** trivia
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

Q is the only letter of the English alphabet that doesn't appear in any of the 50 U.S. state names. Every other letter appears at least once:

- X: Texas, New Mexico
- Z: Arizona
- J: New Jersey
- B: Alabama, Nebraska
- etc.

## Why It Matters

Classic trivia question. Good bar bet material.

## Quiz Question

What's the only letter that doesn't appear in any U.S. state name?

## Answer

Q
