# *NSYNC

**Topic:** music
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

***NSYNC** - boy band active 1995-2002. Members: Justin Timberlake, JC Chasez, Chris Kirkpatrick, Joey Fatone, Lance Bass.

Hits: "Bye Bye Bye," "It's Gonna Be Me," "Tearin' Up My Heart," "I Want You Back."

Rival to Backstreet Boys in the late '90s boy band wars. Justin Timberlake went on to a huge solo career.

## Quiz Question

What boy band had members Justin Timberlake, JC Chasez, Chris Kirkpatrick, Joey Fatone, and Lance Bass?

## Answer

*NSYNC.
