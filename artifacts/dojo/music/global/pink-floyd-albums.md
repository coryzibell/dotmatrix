# Pink Floyd - Dark Side / The Wall

**Topic:** music
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Pink Floyd** - British prog rock band. Released *The Dark Side of the Moon* (1973) and *The Wall* (1979).

Dark Side of the Moon stayed on the Billboard 200 for 937 weeks. The Wall gave us "Another Brick in the Wall" and the iconic marching hammers imagery.

Members: Roger Waters, David Gilmour, Nick Mason, Richard Wright, Syd Barrett (early).

## Quiz Question

What British rock band released the albums Dark Side of the Moon and The Wall?

## Answer

Pink Floyd.
