# Beyoncé - Destiny's Child

**Topic:** music
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Destiny's Child** was Beyoncé's group before her solo career. Final lineup: Beyoncé Knowles, Kelly Rowland, Michelle Williams.

Active 1990-2006. Hits: "Say My Name," "Survivor," "Bootylicious," "Bills, Bills, Bills."

One of the best-selling girl groups of all time (~60 million records).

## Quiz Question

What band was Beyoncé in before going solo?

## Answer

Destiny's Child.
