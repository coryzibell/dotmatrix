# Madonna - Queen of Pop

**Topic:** music
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Madonna** has been called the "Queen of Pop" since the 1980s. Best-selling female recording artist of all time.

Hits: "Like a Virgin," "Material Girl," "Vogue," "Like a Prayer," "Hung Up."

Other royal titles:
- King of Pop: Michael Jackson
- Queen of Soul: Aretha Franklin
- King of Rock and Roll: Elvis Presley

## Quiz Question

What singer is known as the "Queen of Pop"?

## Answer

Madonna.
