# Tungsten's Chemical Symbol

**Topic:** chemistry
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

Tungsten has the chemical symbol 'W' from its German name, Wolfram. The English name "tungsten" comes from Swedish for "heavy stone."

Tungsten has the highest melting point of any element (3,422Â°C).

## Why It Matters

- Used in light bulb filaments, welding electrodes, and armor-piercing ammunition
- Other elements with non-obvious symbols: Na (sodium/natrium), K (potassium/kalium), Fe (iron/ferrum)
- The Wolfram language/company is named after this element

## Quiz Question

What element has the chemical symbol 'W'?

## Answer

Tungsten (from Wolfram).
