# 30 Rock - TGS

**Topic:** sitcoms (2000s-2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**TGS with Tracy Jordan** is the fictional sketch show in 30 Rock.

**Details:**
- Originally called "The Girlie Show"
- Renamed when Tracy Jordan (Tracy Morgan) joined the cast
- Films at 30 Rockefeller Plaza (hence the show's name)
- Liz Lemon (Tina Fey) is the head writer
- Jack Donaghy (Alec Baldwin) is the NBC executive overseeing it

**Main cast of TGS:**
- Tracy Jordan - unpredictable movie star
- Jenna Maroney - desperate for fame
- Writers include Frank, Toofer, Lutz

**The show:**
- Ran 2006-2013 on NBC
- Created by Tina Fey
- Based on her SNL experiences

## Quiz Question

In 30 Rock, what is the name of the fictional sketch comedy show where Liz Lemon works?

## Answer

TGS with Tracy Jordan (originally "The Girlie Show").
