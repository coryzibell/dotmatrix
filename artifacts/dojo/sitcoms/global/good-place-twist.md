# The Good Place - Season 1 Twist

**Topic:** sitcoms (2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**The Season 1 twist** in The Good Place is that the "Good Place" is actually the Bad Place.

**The reveal:**
- Eleanor figures out that they're being tortured
- The whole neighborhood is a psychological experiment
- Michael (Ted Danson) is actually a demon
- He designed it so the four humans would torture each other
- Eleanor's famous line: "THIS is the Bad Place!"

**The setup:**
- Eleanor knows she doesn't belong in the Good Place
- She was a bad person who got in by mistake (or so she thinks)
- The other residents - Chidi, Tahani, Jason - all have their own issues
- They were specifically grouped to annoy each other

**The show:**
- Ran 2016-2020 on NBC
- Created by Michael Schur (Parks and Rec, Brooklyn 99)
- Explores philosophy and ethics

## Quiz Question

In The Good Place, what does Eleanor discover about the "Good Place" at the end of Season 1?

## Answer

It's actually the Bad Place - the whole thing is a torture experiment designed to make them torment each other.
