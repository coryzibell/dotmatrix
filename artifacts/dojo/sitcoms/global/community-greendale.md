# Community - Greendale

**Topic:** sitcoms (2000s-2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Greendale Community College** is the setting for Community.

**Details:**
- Located in Colorado
- Slogan: "You're already accepted!"
- Mascot: The Human Being (a creepy, non-offensive grey humanoid)
- Dean: Craig Pelton (obsessed with Jeff Winger)
- Known for being a terrible school

**The study group:**
- Jeff Winger - disbarred lawyer
- Britta Perry - activist
- Abed Nadir - pop culture obsessive
- Troy Barnes - former jock
- Shirley Bennett - religious mom
- Annie Edison - overachiever
- Pierce Hawthorne - old rich guy

**The show:**
- Ran 2009-2015 (NBC, then Yahoo)
- Created by Dan Harmon
- Famous for meta humor and themed episodes

## Quiz Question

In Community, what is the name of the fictional college where the study group meets?

## Answer

Greendale Community College.
