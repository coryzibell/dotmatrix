# The Office - Dunder Mifflin

**Topic:** sitcoms (2000s-2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Dunder Mifflin** is the paper company in The Office (US).

**Details:**
- Full name: Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Inc.
- Scranton, Pennsylvania branch is the main setting
- Slogan: "Dunder Mifflin, the people person's paper people"
- Regional Manager: Michael Scott (later Andy Bernard, then Dwight Schrute)
- Eventually bought by Sabre, then became independent again

**The show:**
- Ran 2005-2013 on NBC
- Based on the UK Office by Ricky Gervais
- Mockumentary style
- Stars Steve Carell as Michael Scott

## Quiz Question

In The Office (US), what is the name of the paper company where the main characters work?

## Answer

Dunder Mifflin (Paper Company).
