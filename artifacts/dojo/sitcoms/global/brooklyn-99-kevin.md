# Brooklyn Nine-Nine - Kevin

**Topic:** sitcoms (2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Kevin Cozner** is Captain Holt's husband in Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

**Character details:**
- Played by Marc Evan Jackson
- Professor of classics at Columbia University
- Just as formal and deadpan as Holt
- They have a dog named Cheddar (a corgi)
- Their ultra-serious relationship is a recurring joke

**Running gags:**
- Both speak in monotone, formal language
- Their idea of fun is extremely boring to others
- Kevin initially dislikes Jake, later warms up
- Cheddar the corgi is treated like royalty

**The show:**
- Ran 2013-2021 (Fox, then NBC)
- Stars Andy Samberg as Jake Peralta
- Andre Braugher as Captain Raymond Holt

## Quiz Question

In Brooklyn Nine-Nine, what is the name of Captain Holt's husband?

## Answer

Kevin Cozner.
