# Parks and Recreation - Pawnee

**Topic:** sitcoms (2000s-2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Pawnee, Indiana** is the fictional town where Parks and Recreation is set.

**Details:**
- Small mid-sized Indiana town
- Known for obesity, raccoon problems, and bizarre local history
- Has a bitter rivalry with neighboring town Eagleton
- Slogan: "First in Friendship, Fourth in Obesity"
- Famous for Sweetums candy factory

**Notable Pawnee things:**
- Lil' Sebastian (beloved miniature horse)
- The Harvest Festival
- JJ's Diner (waffles!)
- The Pit (later becomes a park)

**The show:**
- Ran 2009-2015 on NBC
- Stars Amy Poehler as Leslie Knope
- Created by Greg Daniels and Michael Schur

## Quiz Question

In Parks and Recreation, what is the name of the fictional Indiana town where the show is set?

## Answer

Pawnee.
