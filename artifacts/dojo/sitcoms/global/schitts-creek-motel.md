# Schitt's Creek - Rosebud Motel

**Topic:** sitcoms (2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Rosebud Motel** is where the Rose family lives in Schitt's Creek.

**Details:**
- Run-down motel in the town of Schitt's Creek
- The Rose family moves there after losing their fortune
- Johnny and Moira share one room, David and Alexis share another
- Johnny eventually buys and renovates the motel
- Named after Alexis's middle name (Rosebud)

**The premise:**
- The Rose family (video store magnates) loses everything to fraud
- Their only remaining asset is the town of Schitt's Creek (bought as a joke)
- They're forced to live in the motel while rebuilding their lives

**The show:**
- Ran 2015-2020 on CBC/Pop TV
- Created by Eugene Levy and Dan Levy
- Swept the 2020 Emmys

## Quiz Question

In Schitt's Creek, what is the name of the motel where the Rose family is forced to live?

## Answer

Rosebud Motel.
