# New Girl - Douchebag Jar

**Topic:** sitcoms (2010s)
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**The Douchebag Jar** is a running gag in New Girl.

**How it works:**
- Schmidt has to put money in the jar whenever he says or does something douchey
- The roommates enforce it
- Schmidt fills it up constantly with his vain, pretentious behavior
- The jar is always full

**Schmidt's douchey moments include:**
- Bragging about his body
- Using pretentious vocabulary
- Being overly metrosexual
- Making self-absorbed comments

**The show:**
- Ran 2011-2018 on Fox
- Stars Zooey Deschanel as Jess
- Max Greenfield as Schmidt
- The loft roommates: Jess, Nick, Schmidt, Winston, and Coach

## Quiz Question

In New Girl, what is the name of the jar where Schmidt has to put money for certain behaviors?

## Answer

The Douchebag Jar.
