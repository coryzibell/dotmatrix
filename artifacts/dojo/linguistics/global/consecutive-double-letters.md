# Consecutive Double Letters

**Topic:** linguistics
**Scope:** global
**Date:** 2025-11-30

## Knowledge

**Bookkeeper** (and bookkeeping) is the only common English word with three consecutive double letters: b-**oo**-**kk**-**ee**-per.

Mississippi has three pairs of double letters (ss, ss, pp) but they're not consecutive - there are single letters between them.

## Why It Matters

- Classic word puzzle / trivia question
- Good example of how English orthography can create unusual patterns
- "Subbookkeeper" would have four consecutive double letters, but it's not a real word

## Quiz Question

What common English word has three consecutive double letters?

## Answer

Bookkeeper (oo-kk-ee).
